Welcome to the Wee Bag Bands website!

Based in North Wales, The Wee Bag Band's raison d'être is to bring mad, bad, trad, popular and contemporary diddly, diddly Irish/Celtic music and song to the masses at pubs, clubs, doos 'n festivals in North Wales, UK, infinity & beyond...

We must be hitting the notes in the right order as to date our music has taken us to many parts of the world including the UK, France, Switzerland, Spain, Germany, USA, Cuba, Bahamas, Curacao, Puerto Rico, Honduras and even northern Greenland!

Looking for a Ceilidh? Then look no further! Alan our bodhran player and Paul on guitar are also dance callers!
